About Us

Welcome to Northshore Fix! I'm Wesley Williams, and I've been serving the Covington, Louisiana area for over 15 years with quality handyman and general construction services.

From small repairs to complete remodels, I take pride in delivering craftsmanship that exceeds expectations. Whether you need a quick fix or a major renovation, no job is too small for our team.

As a locally owned and operated business, I understand the unique needs of Northshore homeowners. I'm committed to honest pricing, reliable service, and building lasting relationships with my clients.
